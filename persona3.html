Name: Anthony Verklushberg
Job: Electronic Technician

Anthony is a senior electronic technician at I-Buy-Mobi (IBM) of over 5 years. IBM’s offers a main service of refurbishing and recycling old phones and electronic devices. Anthony's main duty in the company is to investigate claims of shoddy performance of electronic devices after a certain amount of time after purchasing the device. From collected data, almost 50% of the electronics deposited at IBM are in perfect condition, with the only complaint that they are “out of date” and are slower than more recent designs. Anthony believes that these old devices can be refurbished and reused in different environments, but the CEO believes that they should all be sent to landfill. Anthony wishes to find an alternative.
